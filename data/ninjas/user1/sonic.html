Sonic Mania


Information:

Sonic Mania is a new sonic game in the style of the classic 2d sonic games.


Pros:

Fun Platforming Gameplay

Competitive Multiplayer


Cons:

Multiplayer Splitscreen stretching looks off.

If you get a Game Over you have to restart the whole game!


My thoughts:

I think that this game is like the orignal sonic games but with better level design. If you are considering trying out sonic games I recommend that you try this game.


Final Score: 7/10
